# SovaKeep — Support

Need help, found a bug, or have a feature idea?

## Contact

Email us at **<sovakeep@gmail.com>**, or open an issue on the support tracker:
**<https://github.com/chiffad/sovakeep-legal/issues>**

When reporting a problem or writing in, please include:

- Your device model and OS version (e.g. iPhone 14, iOS 18.2)
- The app version (More → Settings)
- What you did, what you expected, and what happened instead

Please **never** post budgets, account balances, or other personal financial
information in an issue — the tracker is public, and we don't need that data
to help you.

## Your data

SovaKeep stores your data on your device only. For details, see the
[Privacy Policy](privacy.html).
